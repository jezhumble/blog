This is my personal blog. My professional bio is on my Continuous Delivery site.

I live in the San Francisco Bay Area, work at 18F, and teach at the iSchool at UC Berkeley. In my spare time I write and read music and books.

I have a degree in physics and philosophy from Oxford and a masters in ethnomusicology from the School of Oriental and African Studies, University of London, for which I studied Hindi and Indian classical music.

I'm married to Rani Sanghera, and we have two daughters.
